Greenvale Park Homework Center
Our Mission


To assist adolescents (ages 12-18) with school assessments or assignments, develop their social and personal skills, and foster their academic involvement.


Services Include:

  • After-school homework support for core subjects - Mathematics, English, Sciences, and Information and Communication Technology (ICT).
  • Access to computers with internet capabilities, and WIFI for devices
  • Study groups in the core subjects
  • Free Workshops
  • Experienced tutors

Operating Hours:

  • Monday to Friday 3 - 7 pm
